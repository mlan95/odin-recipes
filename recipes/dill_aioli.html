Dill Aioli

Description

This dill aioli makes a flavourful condiment that goes great with salmon, chicken, potatoes, burgers or a dipping sauce for fries. It's a simple recipe consisting of four ingrediants and takes less than five minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup mayo
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 3 tablespoons fresh dill weed or 1 tablespoon dried dill

Steps

  1. Start by chopping the dill, mincing the garlic and juicing the lemon.
  2. In a small bowl, add the mayo, lemon juice, dill and garlic.
  3. Mix together until well blended. Cover and place in fridge to chill for 15 minutes or more so that the flavors can blend.
